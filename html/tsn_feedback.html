FAQs

Q1. How do I apply to Singapore Polytechnic?

A1. The students can apply through JAE, JPAE, DAE, EAE, PFP. For more information: click here

Q2. Is it possible for the student to change his course after studying in SP for a few months?

A2. The student cannot change course once the semester has started.

Q3. Are there any opportunities for overseas exposure?

A3. There are many opportunities for SP students to travel overseas for educational or community service experiences.

Q4. What is the tuition fee like?

A4. The tuition fee for academic year 2021/22 for Singaporean full-time students is $2,900.00.
